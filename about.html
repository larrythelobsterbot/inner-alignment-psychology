About Us

Our Approach

Ann-Marie Trinh

BPsych (Hons), MPsych (Prof) with first class honours (2018)
Full registration with AHPRA

Ann-Marie is a registered psychologist who brings warmth, empathy, and a deep commitment to understanding each person's unique story. With extensive experience in trauma-informed care, she creates a therapeutic space that feels both safe and gently challenging.

Her background spans a diverse range of contexts — including educational, occupational, community, Defence, and not-for-profit settings — giving her a nuanced appreciation of the many ways that environment, systems, and the demands of everyday life can shape our inner world.

Her approach is grounded in the belief that meaningful change happens when we honour the whole person — not just symptoms, but the complex interplay of mind, body, relationships, and lived experience. She draws on evidence-based modalities while remaining curious about the subtle dimensions of human experience — intuition, consciousness, and the inner wisdom that arises from reflection — tailoring each session to what feels right for you.

Outside of her work, Ann-Marie enjoys meditation, time in nature, writing, and cultivating a life that balances reflection, growth, and purposeful action.
