Medicare & Mental Health Care Plans

Accessing a Medicare Rebate

If you have a Mental Health Care Plan (MHCP) from your GP, you may be eligible for a Medicare rebate on up to 10 individual sessions per calendar year. The rebate amount is set by Medicare and will be applied after your session fee is paid in full.

To access a rebate, you will need a current referral from your GP along with a Mental Health Care Plan. We recommend speaking with your GP about whether a MHCP is appropriate for you.

How It Works

1. Visit your GP and request a Mental Health Care Plan and referral to a psychologist.

2. Book your initial appointment with us. Please bring your referral and MHCP to your first session.

3. After your session, we will process your Medicare claim on the spot so the rebate is returned to your nominated account.

Private Health Insurance

If you have private health insurance with extras cover, you may be able to claim a rebate depending on your level of cover. Please check with your fund for details about your entitlement.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that life can be unpredictable. If you need to cancel or reschedule, we kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice. Late cancellations or missed appointments may incur a cancellation fee of 50% of the session cost, as the time has been reserved exclusively for you.

Questions?

We're Happy to Help

If you have questions about fees, rebates, or the referral process, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to make the process as straightforward as possible.
