Welcome

Inner Alignment Psychology offers a whole-person approach to therapy, honouring the connection between mind, body, and lived experience. We create a safe, grounded space to explore what matters most, where insight, clarity, and meaningful change can unfold.

How We Work

Guiding Principles

Person-Centred

You are the expert on your own life. Therapy is a collaborative process where your goals, pace, and comfort guide the way. We meet you where you are, without judgement.

Trauma-Informed

We understand that past experiences shape the present. Our approach prioritises safety, choice, and empowerment — working gently with the nervous system to support healing.

Holistic & Integrative

True wellbeing involves mind, body, and spirit. We draw on multiple evidence-based modalities to create a tailored approach that honours your whole experience.

Our Values

A Safe Space for You

At Inner Alignment Psychology, we believe that every person deserves a space where they can be truly seen and heard. Our practice is built on the values of safety, respect, inclusivity, and genuine human connection.

We honour diversity in all its forms and are committed to creating a non-judgemental environment where you feel comfortable to explore difficult emotions, challenge old patterns, and discover new ways of being.

Therapy is not about having all the answers — it's about creating the conditions for understanding, growth, and meaningful change to unfold naturally.
